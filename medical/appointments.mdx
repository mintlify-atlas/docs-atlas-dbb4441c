# Book an appointment

Scheduling an appointment with Dr. Kothari is straightforward. The practice offers both in-person consultations and telemedicine appointments.

<Info>
  Visit [indoredoctor.com](https://indoredoctor.com/) to schedule your appointment.
</Info>

## How to schedule

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the website">
    Go to [indoredoctor.com](https://indoredoctor.com/) to access the appointment scheduling system.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose appointment type">
    Select whether you need an in-person visit or a telemedicine consultation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select date and time">
    Choose from available appointment slots that work with your schedule.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm your appointment">
    Complete the booking and receive confirmation of your scheduled appointment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Telemedicine provides access to Dr. Kothari's expertise through virtual consultations. This option offers convenience for patients who prefer remote care or have difficulty traveling.

Telemedicine appointments are available for various medical concerns that can be addressed remotely, providing convenient access to healthcare from your home.
