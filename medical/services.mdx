## Complete physical examinations

Regular physical examinations are essential for maintaining good health and detecting potential health issues early.

### What to expect

A complete physical examination at the practice includes a thorough evaluation of your overall health status, helping establish a baseline for your wellness and identifying any areas of concern.

<Note>
  Physical examinations provide comprehensive health assessments tailored to your individual needs.
</Note>

## Chronic disease management

Living with a chronic condition requires ongoing care, monitoring, and support. Dr. Kothari provides chronic disease management services to help patients effectively manage their conditions and maintain quality of life.

### Comprehensive care approach

The practice offers personalized treatment plans and regular monitoring to help you manage chronic health conditions effectively.

## Preventive healthcare

Prevention is a cornerstone of good health. The practice's preventive healthcare services help you stay healthy by identifying risk factors and addressing potential problems before they develop.

### Preventive services

Preventive care includes health screenings, lifestyle counseling, and proactive health management to keep you and your family healthy.

## Emergency consultations

Medical concerns that require prompt attention can be addressed through emergency consultation services. The practice provides urgent care for acute medical problems.

### When to seek emergency consultation

Emergency consultations are available for urgent medical matters that need immediate professional attention.

<Warning>
  For life-threatening emergencies, call emergency services immediately or go to the nearest emergency room.
</Warning>

## Telemedicine appointments

Access quality healthcare from anywhere through telemedicine services. Virtual consultations provide a convenient alternative to in-person visits for many medical concerns.

### Benefits of telemedicine

- **Convenience** - Consult from home or work
- **Accessibility** - Healthcare access for patients with transportation challenges
- **Time-saving** - No travel required
- **Continuity of care** - Easy follow-ups and monitoring
