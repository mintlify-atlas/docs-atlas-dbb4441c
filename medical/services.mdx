# Medical services

Dr. Kothari's practice offers comprehensive medical services designed to address all aspects of your health and wellness. From routine preventive care to managing complex chronic conditions, we provide the full spectrum of primary care services with expertise and compassion.

## Complete physical examinations

Regular physical examinations are the cornerstone of maintaining good health. Our comprehensive exams help detect potential health issues early and establish a baseline for your overall wellness.

### What's included

A complete physical examination at our practice includes:

- **Vital signs assessment** - Blood pressure, heart rate, temperature, and respiratory rate monitoring
- **Physical inspection** - Thorough examination of all body systems including cardiovascular, respiratory, and neurological
- **Medical history review** - Discussion of current medications, past illnesses, surgeries, and family health history
- **Laboratory tests** - Blood work and other diagnostic tests as needed based on your age, risk factors, and symptoms
- **Health counseling** - Personalized advice on nutrition, exercise, and lifestyle modifications

<Info>
  Annual physical examinations are recommended for adults to maintain optimal health and catch any concerns early.
</Info>

### When to schedule

<Accordion title="Annual wellness exams">
  Schedule a comprehensive physical examination once a year to monitor your health status, update vaccinations, and discuss any health concerns that have developed since your last visit.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="New patient evaluations">
  If you're new to our practice, schedule an initial comprehensive examination so Dr. Kothari can establish your health baseline and understand your medical needs.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Pre-employment or pre-surgery clearance">
  We provide medical examinations required for employment, insurance, or surgical clearance purposes.
</Accordion>

## Chronic disease management

Living with a chronic condition requires ongoing care, monitoring, and support. Dr. Kothari specializes in helping patients effectively manage chronic diseases to improve quality of life and prevent complications.

### Conditions we manage

Our chronic disease management services include:

- **Diabetes mellitus** - Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes management with blood sugar monitoring, medication adjustment, and lifestyle counseling
- **Hypertension** - Blood pressure management through medication, dietary changes, and regular monitoring
- **Asthma and COPD** - Respiratory condition management with inhaler therapy and lung function monitoring
- **Thyroid disorders** - Hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism treatment with hormone level monitoring
- **Heart disease** - Cardiovascular disease management and risk reduction strategies
- **Arthritis** - Pain management and mobility support for osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis

### Our management approach

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initial assessment">
    Comprehensive evaluation of your condition, current treatment, and health status to establish a management baseline.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Personalized treatment plan">
    Development of a customized care plan that includes medications, lifestyle modifications, and monitoring schedules.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Regular monitoring">
    Scheduled follow-up appointments to track your progress, adjust treatments, and monitor for complications.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Patient education">
    Ongoing education about your condition, self-management techniques, and when to seek immediate care.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Coordination of care">
    Collaboration with specialists when needed and coordination of all aspects of your healthcare.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Consistent management of chronic conditions can significantly improve your quality of life and reduce the risk of serious complications.
</Note>

## Emergency consultations

Medical emergencies can happen at any time. Dr. Kothari provides urgent consultation services for acute medical problems that require immediate attention but may not necessitate an emergency room visit.

### When to seek emergency consultation

Schedule an emergency consultation for:

- High fever with severe symptoms
- Acute respiratory infections
- Severe pain or discomfort
- Sudden onset of symptoms
- Minor injuries requiring medical evaluation
- Acute exacerbation of chronic conditions
- Urgent prescription needs

<Info>
  For life-threatening emergencies such as chest pain, difficulty breathing, severe bleeding, or loss of consciousness, call emergency services immediately or go to the nearest emergency room.
</Info>

### Emergency appointment availability

We strive to accommodate urgent cases as quickly as possible. Contact our office to schedule an emergency consultation, and we'll work to see you the same day or within 24 hours depending on the severity of your condition.

## Telemedicine appointments

Access quality healthcare from anywhere through our telemedicine services. Virtual consultations provide a convenient alternative to in-person visits for many medical concerns.

### What can be addressed via telemedicine

- Follow-up appointments for chronic conditions
- Medication refills and adjustments
- Review of test results
- Minor acute illnesses (colds, flu, allergies)
- Skin condition evaluations
- Mental health consultations
- General health questions and advice

### Benefits of telemedicine

- **Convenience** - No travel required; consult from home or work
- **Time-saving** - Reduced waiting times and no commute
- **Accessibility** - Healthcare access for patients with mobility issues or transportation challenges
- **Continuity of care** - Easy follow-ups and regular monitoring of chronic conditions
- **Safety** - Reduced exposure to infectious diseases in waiting rooms
