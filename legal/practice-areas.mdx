## Civil litigation

When disputes cannot be resolved through negotiation, Attorney Singh provides skilled courtroom advocacy to protect your interests in civil litigation matters.

### Litigation services

**Pre-litigation counsel**
Assessment of claims, demand letters, and negotiation attempts to resolve disputes before court filing.

**Court representation**
Experienced advocacy in trial courts, including pleading preparation, discovery, motion practice, and trial representation.

**Appeals**
Appellate representation to challenge unfavorable decisions or defend favorable verdicts.

**Alternative dispute resolution**
Mediation and arbitration representation as cost-effective alternatives to traditional litigation.

### Common civil matters

- Contract disputes
- Property and boundary disputes
- Business conflicts
- Personal injury claims
- Debt collection and creditor rights
- Consumer protection matters
