# Schedule a consultation

Taking the first step toward resolving your legal matter begins with a confidential consultation. Attorney Singh offers consultations to evaluate your situation and discuss your legal options.

<Info>

## Why schedule a consultation

A legal consultation provides you with the opportunity to:

- Discuss your legal situation in confidentiality
- Receive professional assessment of your case
- Understand your legal rights and options
- Get answers to your legal questions
- Decide if Attorney Singh is the right fit for your needs
